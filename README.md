### About

I'm a Master's student in **Quantitative Data Science Methods**, living somewhere
between **machine learning, psychometrics, and econometrics**. I like models
that know what they don't know — so most of my recent work leans into
**probabilistic / Bayesian ML** and latent variable modeling.

When I'm not fitting priors, I'm probably building something around **market
microstructure** or breaking a dataset to see what falls out.

### What I'm into

- Probabilistic & Bayesian ML · uncertainty that isn't just a confidence band
- Latent variable models & psychometrics
- Quant finance, order books, and market microstructure
- Applied deep learning — when it actually beats the baseline

### Toolbox

### Featured Work

**[Orderflow Lab](https://github.com/samuel29102002/orderflow-lab)**
Self-contained market-microstructure simulator — Rust matching engine, DeepLOB
forecaster, Almgren–Chriss execution agent, Next.js dashboard. 2M ops/sec and
0.6 ms inference on a single laptop.

**[LEMON Love Predictor](https://github.com/samuel29102002/LEMON-Love-Predictor)**
Can emotion-regulation, cognition, and behavior predict whether you're in a
relationship? Built on the LEMON dataset — psychometrics meets ML with a
cheekier research question than most.

**[Continuous-Time Derivatives Pricing](https://github.com/samuel29102002/Continuous-Time-Derivatives-Pricing)**
Stochastic calculus in practice — pricing options under continuous-time
dynamics, because closed-form solutions don't simulate themselves.
