### About

I'm a Master's student in **Quantitative Data Science Methods**, focused on the
intersection of **machine learning, psychometrics, and econometrics**. My current
research interests center on **probabilistic machine learning** — Bayesian
inference, latent variable models, and uncertainty quantification in applied
settings.

Alongside coursework in Data Literacy, Deep Learning, and Latent Variable
Modeling, I build projects that put these methods into practice — most recently
around **market microstructure and order-flow analytics**.

### Focus Areas

- Econometric Modeling and Causal Inference
